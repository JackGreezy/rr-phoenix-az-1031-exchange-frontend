# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Principles

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients

- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Use hypothetical examples that illustrate service capabilities
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes

### No Testimonials or Unverifiable Claims

- No testimonials from clients
- No review stars or ratings
- No badges without verifiable sources
- No claims about specific project outcomes or client results

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers

- Educational content only. Not tax, legal, or investment advice.
- A 1031 exchange defers federal and Arizona income tax on qualifying real property.
- It does not remove state or county transfer taxes.
- Users should consult a Qualified Intermediary and tax advisor before acting.

### Securities Disclaimers

- DST or TIC may be securities.
- We do not sell securities.
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only.

### Footer Disclosure

Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.

## Content Guidelines

### Example Capability Format

All example capabilities must include:

- Disclaimer: "Example of the type of engagement we can handle"
- Service type or location
- Situation description
- Our approach
- Expected outcome
- Contact CTA: "Contact us to discuss your situation in [LOCATION]. We can share references upon request."

### Language Requirements

- Plain English
- No contractions
- No em dashes
- Short sentences
- Technical accuracy
- Rank and rent compliant language only

### Location References

- Include city and state abbreviation in required places
- Use consistent location naming
- Reference local market dynamics accurately
- Do not make up information about locations
